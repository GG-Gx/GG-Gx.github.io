Germán Gabriel García is a trumpet player, improviser and composer born in Rosario, Santa Fe, Argentina and currently living in Berlin. He began his trumpet studies privately and later studied classical and jazz music at the Escuela Municipal de Música de Rosario and at the Escuela de Música de la UNR. When he moved to Buenos Aires, he continued his studies at the Manuel de Falla Conservatory. After successive encounters with experimental and improvised music, he became interested in the processes of creative research in this line and developed it actively, investigating and playing in different formations with other musicians in the field of experimental music and Neue Musik in Berlin. He is currently focused on expanding the sounds of the trumpet, investigating techniques, using the possibilities of his body related to sound and gesture and also exploring electric amplification and digital electronics.
